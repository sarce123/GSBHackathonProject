About Brazen Boxes

Brazen Boxes are boxes installed throughout various cities and homeless centers, and are routinely stocked by only Brazen Menstruation volunteers to ensure cleanliness. All materials in the boxes are free for community members! Donations are always welcome, please contact your local representative for more information.

What do Brazen Boxes include?

Brazen Boxes include:

  • Eco-friendly pads and tampons
  • Menstrual cups
  • Empowering quotes

    • Brazen Box Locations

    As of now, Brazen Boxes are only located in the Los Angeles Metropolitan Area; however, we are actively expanding our organization to an international scope.
