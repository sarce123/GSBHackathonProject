Brazen Menstruation




Brazen Menstruation's mission is to empower those who menstruate (nonbinary and transgender inclusive) and to shed light on the pressing issues that low-income individuals face when menstruating. Our website features two fully-functional games that encourage discussions surrounding menstruation, signups for conventions, and a wide variety of resources including solutions for more eco-friendly period products.

INCLUSIVITY

Brazen Menstruation encourages the public eliminate the use of gendered language when referring to menstruation. We prioritize the goal of creating spaces for nonbinary and transgender individuals to share their stories and receive help and materials when needed. Individuals of all genders, not just individuals who identify as women, can menstruate, and we work to empower them all!


STATISTICS

  • 2/3 of low-income individuals in the USA endure period poverty
  • 12% of individuals have had to resort to using or making unsafe period products
  • Over an individual's lifetime, they can easily spend up to $6000 on period products alone
